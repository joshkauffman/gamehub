📖 THE MANUEL
Everything you need to know about Dog Man Dash!
🎮 GAME MODES
Classic: Run through 3 city levels and reach the goal flag! Defeat enemies and collect coins. Unlock new characters!
Survivor Island: Survive endless waves of enemies on a tropical island! The longer you last, the more points you earn.
Tower Defense: Top-down view! Defend Dog Man H.Q. from waves of incoming villains. Defeat enemies to earn coins — spend coins to deploy fighter allies (F key) or place barrier walls (B key)!
🦸 CHARACTERS
Dog Man — Loyal & Brave. Throws BONES and uses Star Power!
Lil' Petey — Smart & Sneaky. Throws YARN BALLS at enemies!
80-HD 🔒 — Robot Warrior. Shoots a LASER that auto-targets enemies! Unlock: Complete Level 1.
Molly 🔒 — Super Sleuth. Uses a SPEECH bubble to put all enemies to sleep! Unlock: Complete Level 2.
Petey 🔒 — The Super Villain! Uses LASER power. Unlock: Earn 8,000 points total.
⌨️ CONTROLS
Arrow Keys or WASD — Move (all directions in Tower Defense!)
SPACE or UP Arrow — Jump (Classic and Survivor modes)
DOWN Arrow or S — Duck! Move faster and avoid tall enemies. (Also moves DOWN in Tower Defense)
SHIFT — Attack! Punch nearby enemies, or throw your power-up item.
F key — Deploy a Fighter ally (Tower Defense only — costs 10 coins)
B key — Place a Barrier wall (Tower Defense only — costs 5 coins)
⭐ POWER-UPS
★ STAR POWER — Golden invincibility! Run into enemies to defeat them instantly!
🦴 BONE — Press SHIFT to throw a bone and knock out an enemy! (Dog Man)
🧶 YARN BALL — Press SHIFT to throw yarn and knock out an enemy! (Lil' Petey)
⚡ LASER — Auto-aims at the nearest enemy and zaps them every few seconds! (80-HD, Petey)
💬 SPEECH — Sends all enemies to sleep — they can't attack for 5 seconds! (Molly)
💡 TIPS
Defeat 5 enemies in a row to earn a power-up drop!
In Tower Defense, place barriers to slow enemies down while fighters attack them!
Ducking in Classic mode lets you slide under some enemies!
In Tower Defense, move around the map to intercept enemies before they reach H.Q.!
SPACE/UP=Jump | DOWN=Duck | SHIFT=Attack/Throw | Pick up the Invincibility Star!
